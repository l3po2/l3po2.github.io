How CPvP.gg Works

CPvP.gg is the first Crystal PvP Battle Royale server. Here's everything you need to know before you drop in.

📅

Event Server

CPvP.gg runs scheduled daily sessions. There's no open queue — games are hosted consistently at set times. Check #events in Discord for the next session.

👥

Duos Only

All games are Duos. You can invite your own teammate, or you'll be automatically matched with one if you don't have someone ready.

🌀

Shrinking Storm

The border starts closing the moment the game begins and takes 23 minutes to reach the final circle. The final circle is random every game — no two matches are the same.

📦

Looting

Loot from 3 tiers of chests spread across the map. Rare Airdrops land at random times but only at Cities and POIs — mark them on your mental map.

Loot System

Chest Tiers

Three tiers of chests are scattered around the map. The higher the tier, the better the gear.

Tier 1

Common

Basic resources spread throughout the map. Good for early looting to get started quickly.

Tier 2

Uncommon

Better resources plus +1 Sharpness Books. Stack up to Sharpness 7 on your sword from these.

Tier 3

Rare

High value loot including the Anti-Pearl Stick and Launch Pad. Hunt these down.

✈️
Airdrops — Spawn at random times at Cities & POIs only. Contains the rarest custom items in the game including the mythic Hotbar Scrambler. Check the map below for POI locations.
Arsenal

Custom Items

CPvP.gg has 6 custom items that change how the game is played. Learn them — and learn to play around them.

🪄
Anti-Pearl Stick
TIER 3 CHEST

Hit a player to disable their ender pearls for 5 seconds. A key counter in crystal PvP engagements.

🚀
Launch Pad
TIER 3 CHEST / AIRDROP

Place on the ground and step on it to launch into the air, then glide down. Great for repositioning or getting out of a bad spot.

📖
Sharpness Books
TIER 2 CHEST

Adds +1 Sharpness to your sword per book. Stacks up to Sharpness 7. Grab these early every game.

💨
AIRDROP ONLY
Speed IV
AIRDROP

Right-click for Speed 4 for 10 seconds. Only 2 uses per item — save them for the right moment.

💥
AIRDROP ONLY
Gravity Grenade
AIRDROP

Throw it and launch every player within a 5 block radius into the air. Devastating for disrupting a fight.

🏹
MYTHIC — AIRDROP ONLY
Hotbar Scrambler
AIRDROP

A crossbow with a 2 minute cooldown. Shoot a player to completely scramble their hotbar. The rarest item in the game.

First off, a huge thank you to everyone who has joined. We'd like to extensively explain the server so we can receive your feedback and suggestions.

CPvP.gg is the first Crystal PvP Battle Royale server. We weren't sure we could balance crystal PvP in this format — it's a very different game mode — but here's what we came up with. We think it works.

For now CPvP.gg runs as an event server with daily scheduled sessions. All games are Duos — bring a friend or get auto-matched. The storm closes over 23 minutes to a random final circle. Loot chests and airdrops, use custom items to outplay your opponents, and be the last team standing.

We recommend every player to read #info in Discord before playing. Check #events for the map and upcoming session times.
