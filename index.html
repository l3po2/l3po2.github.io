// Core Mechanics

How It Works

📅

Event Server

CPvP.gg runs scheduled daily games. Sessions are hosted consistently — check Discord for the next event time.

👥

Duos

All games are Duos. Bring a teammate or get auto-assigned one. No solo queue — teamwork is part of the game.

🌀

Shrinking Storm

The border starts closing the moment a game begins. It takes 23 minutes to reach the final circle — which lands in a random spot every match.

📦

Loot System

Scavenge 3 tiers of chests across the map. Airdrops spawn at Cities & POIs at random times for the best loot.

// Loot Tiers

Chests & Airdrops

TIER 1

Common

Spawns throughout the map with minimal resources. Good for early game basics.

TIER 2

Uncommon

More resources plus +1 Sharpness Books to upgrade your sword up to Sharpness 7.

TIER 3

Rare

High-value loot including the Anti-Pearl Stick and Launch Pad.

✈️

Airdrops

Spawn at random times at Cities & POIs only. Contains the rarest items in the game. Check the map in #events to see POI locations.

// Arsenal

Custom Items

🪄

Anti-Pearl Stick

📦 TIER 3 CHEST

Hit a player to disable their ender pearls for 5 seconds. A crucial counter in crystal PvP engagements.

🚀

Launch Pad

📦 TIER 3 CHEST  /  ✈️ AIRDROP

Place on the ground and step on it to launch into the air, then glide down. Perfect for repositioning or escaping fights.

📖

Sharpness Books

📦 TIER 2 CHEST

Each book adds +1 Sharpness to your sword. Stack up to Sharpness 7 for a serious damage edge.

💨

Speed IV

✈️ AIRDROP ONLY

Right-click for Speed 4 for 10 seconds. Only 2 uses — save them for clutch moments.

💥

Gravity Grenade

✈️ AIRDROP ONLY

Throw to launch every player within a 5 block radius into the air. Great for disrupting fights mid-crystal.

🏹

Hotbar Scrambler

✈️ AIRDROP ONLY — MYTHIC

A crossbow with a 2 minute cooldown. Shoot a player to completely scramble their hotbar at the worst possible moment.

We Hit 1,000 Members 🎉

First off, a huge thank you to everyone who has joined. We'd like to extensively explain the server so we can receive your feedback and suggestions.

CPvP.gg is the first Crystal PvP Battle Royale server. We initially thought we could never balance something like this — crystal PvP is a very different game mode — but here's what we came up with.

For now CPvP.gg is an event server, meaning there will be scheduled games every day hosted consistently. All games are Duos — invite a teammate or get auto-assigned one. The storm closes over 23 minutes to a final random circle. Loot chests scattered across the map, and hunt down airdrops at Cities & POIs for the best gear.

We recommend every player to read #info in Discord before playing. Check #events for the map and upcoming game times.
